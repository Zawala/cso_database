A Brief Guide

Nagivate To Page

To explore our collection of organizations, you can either click on the link "Organizations" in the navigation menu above or use the navigation bar at the top of the page.

Apply filters On Organizations

To find the specific organization you're interested in, use the filters available on the page. You can narrow down your search by:

  • Using the name filter to search for organizations with specific names
  • Applying thematic area filters to focus on organizations working in particular areas
  • Selecting registration type filters to view organizations based on their registration status
These filters will help you efficiently locate and view the organizations that match your criteria.

Contact Organisation

After selecting an organization, click on it to view more details. If their goals and objectives match what you're seeking, use the provided contact information to reach out to them
